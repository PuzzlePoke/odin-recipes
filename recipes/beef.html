Korean Fried Beef

Description

This recipe has been passed down through generations and is a staple beef dish for Koreans.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb of Beef of your choice
  • Soy Sauce
  • Seasonings of your choice
  • Hot Sauce

Steps

  1. Prepare beef in salt brine
  2. Prepare sauce in pan
  3. Mix beef and sauce in pan and enjoy
