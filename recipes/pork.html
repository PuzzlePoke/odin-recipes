Korean Fried Pork

Description

This is a Korean Fried Pork Cutlet recipe passed down through generations and is a staple pork dish that is enjoyed for lunch or dinner

Ingredients

  • 1 lb of pork
  • Panko crumbs
  • Oil
  • Seasonings of Choice

Steps

  1. Prepare pork in salt brine for an hour minimum
  2. Put pork in wet and then dry bath
  3. Fry pork in oil for 5-10 minutes
